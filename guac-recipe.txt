Ingredients:
	- 4 Avocados
	- 1 tomato
	- 1/2 chopped red onion
	- 1/4 clove of garlic
	- 2 Tblspoon salt
	- lemon juice
	- jalapenos

Directions
 1) chop all tomatoes, onions, jalapenoes, and garlic to very small pieces
 2) mash avocadoes with the chopped vegetables
 3) mix in the lemon juice and salt and stir
